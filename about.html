Casey Promise Thompson is a Nashville-based mixed media artist known for emotionally driven, photorealistic portraits that explore personal conflict, resolution, and identity. Her work often uses pencil alongside mixed media and dimensional elements like shadowboxes, blending technical drawing skill with an intentionally raw, impulsive, and sometimes street-art-inspired aesthetic. Critics note both her expressive imprecision and strong draftsmanship, with pieces that feel intimate, ambiguous, and visually striking.

She has gained recognition as a rising figure in the Nashville art scene, earning awards such as multiple ArtSlant Prize honors and being named a favorite local artist by Out & About Magazine. Her exhibitions include solo and group shows, notably at The Red Arrow Gallery.

Educated at Watkins College of Art, Design & Film and trained further in Santa Fe, she also has a long professional background in custom art framing. Her work emphasizes storytelling through organic design, varied materials, and abstraction, aiming to communicate deeply without words.
